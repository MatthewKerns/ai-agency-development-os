## Requirements Gathering Questions (For Emma Call)

### Business Context
1. What's the client's business? Industry?
2. What problem are they trying to solve with email automation?
3. What's their current process? How many hours/week do they spend?
4. What's the volume? (emails/day, emails/week)
5. Why now? What triggered this need?

### Functional Requirements
6. Gmail or Outlook?
7. Single inbox or multiple?
8. What email types need handling? (inquiries, support, scheduling, etc.)
9. What should happen with each type? (auto-reply, route, log, escalate?)
10. Do they need tone-matched responses or templated?
11. Integration with existing CRM or tools?

### Technical Requirements
12. Who needs access to the email log?
13. What data needs to be captured? (sender, subject, classification, summary?)
14. Response time expectations? (instant, within hours, next day?)
15. Any compliance/security requirements?

### Success Criteria
16. How will they measure success?
17. What does "done" look like?
18. What's their budget range?
19. Timeline expectations?

### Scope Boundaries
20. What's explicitly OUT of scope?
21. Phase 1 vs Phase 2 priorities?
