### What Worked Well
1. **Phased delivery model:** Collect progressively, reduce risk for client
2. **Clear deliverables:** Trevor knew exactly what Phase 2 included
3. **Payment on delivery:** No invoice delays, immediate payment
4. **Professional handoff:** Testing completed, demo ready, client confident

2. **Testing before delivery:** Professional QA prevents post-delivery issues
3. **Clear follow-up plan:** Trevor knows his action items, I know mine
