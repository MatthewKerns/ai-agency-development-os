## What is Business DNA?

The foundational elements that define your business:
- **Who:** Target audience (ICP)
- **What:** Services/offerings
- **Why:** Mission and values
- **How:** Unique approach/methodology
- **Where:** Market positioning
- **When:** Customer journey stages
