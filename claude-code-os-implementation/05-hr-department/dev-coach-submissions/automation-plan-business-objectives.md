## Pricing Strategy Questions

### 1. Fixed Price vs Cost-Plus

**When should I use each model?**

**Fixed Price:**
- Pros: Higher perceived value, margin opportunity
- Cons: Risk if scope expands, need accurate estimation
- Best for: Well-defined projects, proven solutions

**Cost-Plus:**
- Pros: Transparent, lower risk, builds trust
- Cons: Visible margins, less profit opportunity
- Best for: Complex projects, new solutions, ongoing work

**Hybrid:**
- Discovery/scoping: Fixed
- Development: Cost-plus
- Best of both worlds?

**QUESTION:** Which model do you recommend for automation projects?
